kayak ride

Our kayak tour lasts approximately 1 hour, it is a very fun ride, and if you do not have experience in rowing, we have a team that teaches you each step. Kayaking is a fun activity that allows you to enjoy nature and the water at the same time. You can paddle through rivers and streams, explore the crystal clear waters of lakes, and enjoy the tranquility of the water surrounded by impressive mountains. Also, kayaking is a great way to exercise and improve your physical condition. Rowing will strengthen your arms, shoulders, back, and core, allowing you to build stamina and burn calories while enjoying the fresh air and natural beauty of the mountains. So you have no way to miss this great adventure!

Climbing has different levels of difficulty to climb, in addition, it is an activity that requires skill, strength and concentration. When climbing you need to consider your balance, technique and safety as you need to make sure you are properly anchored at all times. Camping

Camping: The mountains are a perfect place to camp and enjoy nature, in addition, it will allow you to enjoy impressive views and spectacular landscapes that you will not be able to find anywhere else. From majestic mountain peaks to forested valleys, pure nature will surround you, giving you a sense of peace and tranquility that you won't find in the city. The activity begins at 8 in the evening and ends at 10:30 in the morning. The organizers light a bonfire at 10 and while the food is prepared they tell stories. Do not miss this unforgettable experience!

snowboarding

snowboarding: As you already know, the mountains are an ideal place to practice snow sports, such as snowboarding. One of the most exciting things about snowboarding is that you get to choose your own path on the mountain, whether it's riding down the carefully groomed slopes or venturing off the beaten path to explore more challenging terrain. In addition, snowboarding also offers a great opportunity to make friends and socialize. If you are a beginner, don't worry, we have teachers who will explain the techniques and will always give you the right equipment.

guided walk

Wildlife Observation: The mountains are a unique ecosystem that is home to a wide variety of flora and fauna species, from trees, shrubs and flowers to animals such as bears, deer, birds of prey, lizards and many more. You can spend hours exploring the different trails and paths to find a large number of species. If you like nature, you can take a guided walk to observe the flora and fauna of the area, and photograph wild animals and birds. It is important to note that when you explore you must do so responsibly and respectfully. You must make sure that you do not interfere with the animals or destroy the natural habitat of the plants.

$9.00

mountain biking: An exciting and fun activity that you can enjoy in the mountains is mountain biking. The mountains offer a wide variety of trails and bike paths, ranging from easy routes to more challenging paths that will require increased skill and skill on the bike. Mountain trails also allow you to experience a variety of terrains such as forests, rivers, meadows, and hills, making the experience exciting and interesting. Finally, mountain biking gives you the opportunity to enjoy nature and disconnect from technology and the distractions of everyday life. By being in contact with nature and breathing fresh air, you will feel calmer and more relaxed. You will be provided with the necessary equipment, in addition to the bicycle.

our history

We are a company that has been working since 2000. We founded Travelers because we are a family that loves the mountains and all the activities that can be done. In addition, we think about the satisfaction that they can generate to one and how fun it can be. So what better idea than to create a recreational space and enable activities with work teams and professionals. We got to work and began to design each activity with its specific equipment, we built a dock and bought 15 kayaks. We bought bicycles with equipment to ride and also snowboards. We hire a team of professionals so that they can carry out the activities and that clients feel safe in what they do. Although at the beginning we were the guides since we did not have enough capital to hire a team. So now you know how we started on this adventure!
